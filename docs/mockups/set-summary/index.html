Set summary overlay — mockup gallery

Ten visual proposals for the new "set summary" overlay (replaces the scoreboard between sets in a 16:9 streaming feed). All ten render at 1280 × 720 and are self-contained — open each file in a browser, pick a direction (or ask for variants), and the runtime implementation under overlay_static/ will adopt that style.

All ten share the same demo data so the comparison stays apples-to-apples: Thunder Wolves 25 — Solar Hawks 22, Set 2, duration 4:32, longest streak 6 (TW from 12-12 to 18-12), timeouts 0 / 2, total points 47.
01

Editorial spread

editorial.html

Magazine-style print layout. Serif headline, big score on the right, chart and standfirst in the body, four-column stat footer with hairline rules.

 02

Bento dashboard

bento.html

Modular rounded tiles à la Apple/Stripe. A dominant score tile, a chart tile, and four stat cards filling the 16:9 grid.

 03

Split screen

split-screen.html

Vertical 50/50: left half is info (set, score, footer stats), right half is a full-height point-progression chart with axis labels.

 04

Brand columns

brand-columns.html

Three vertical columns: home team tinted with brand gradient (left), centre with chart and duration, away team tinted (right). Huge per-team score.

 05

Winner podium

podium.html

Winning team raised on a tall pedestal with a crown badge, runner-up shorter alongside, full-width chart ribbon at the bottom.

 06

TV bumper

bumper.html

Broadcast intro-card aesthetic: diagonal team-coloured stripes background, yellow accent corner clips, big "SET RECAP" tag and bold drop-shadow score.

 07

Cinemascope title

cinema.html

Letterboxed movie-title-card feel: black bars top and bottom, serif headline, score as a title card, stats as credits, subtle film grain.

 08

Stadium LED

jumbotron.html

Mimics an arena LED screen: glowing neon team scores, scanline texture, yellow "Set Recap" plate, scrolling marquee ticker at the bottom.

 09

Broadsheet front page

newspaper.html

Newspaper front page: double-rule masthead, three serif columns (chart figure / lede article / scorebox), italic stats footer. Editorial print energy.

 10

Glassmorphism

glass.html

Frosted translucent tiles layered over team-coloured background blobs. Modern OS-style feel — header, score, chart and four stat tiles all "floating".
